# 🕊️ Ultimate Albatross Survival


**A first-person bird flight survival game** — soar as an albatross through a procedurally generated world, discover ancient floating cities, enchant your wings with god-like powers, and survive the dangers below.

> 🌌 **The Low Aether Update is here!** Fly to 1500m and discover the Crystal Metropolis.

---

## 🎮 Play

📥 **Download** the `albatrosssurvival.html` file from this repository and open it in Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.

---

## 🕹️ Complete Controls

| Action | Key |
|--------|-----|
| Lock mouse / look around | Click on game window |
| Steer / move | `W` `A` `S` `D` |
| Takeoff run | Hold `W` + `SPACE` while grounded |
| Glide / regenerate flaps | Hold `SPACE` in air |
| Use flap (boost upward) | `F` (🪶 in hotbar) |
| Use jet boost | `J` (🧨 in hotbar) |
| **Cycle wing fold mode** | `P` (Normal → Folded → Full Dive) |
| **Apply wing enchantment** | `K` (requires ✨ item) |
| Select hotbar slot | `1` – `9` |
| Open backpack | `B` |
| Open crafting (Nest Forge) | `C` |
| Open chests | `E` |

---

## 🪶 Wing Fold System

Press **`P`** to cycle through three flight modes — your glide ratio updates in real-time!

| Mode | Glide Ratio | Effect |
|------|-------------|--------|
| 🕊️ **Normal** | 22:1 | Balanced flight, best glide |
| 🔻 **Folded** | 15:1 | Faster dive, reduced lift |
| ⚡ **Full Dive** | 8.5:1 | Maximum speed, aggressive descent |

*Use wing fold to dive-bomb enemies or escape danger!*

---

## ✨ Enchantment System

Find an **Enchantment Item** (✨) from Low Aether chests or Aether Enchanters, then press **`K`** to apply a random permanent enchantment:

| Enchantment | Effect |
|-------------|--------|
| **Glide Enchant** | 100:1 glide ratio — soar forever with minimal speed loss |
| **Damage Reduction** | Take only 20% damage from all sources — survive anything |
| **Flap Power** | 3G force on every flap — launch yourself to the stratosphere |

*Enchantments are permanent and game-changing. Choose your moment wisely!*

## ⚔️ Combat & Threats

### Hunting Planes ✈️
- **Spawn during daytime** — the skies are dangerous!
- Chase the player, drop bombs (10 damage)
- Can be rammed or attacked with tools
- 1000 HP — takes effort to bring down

### Hunters 👹
- **Spawn at night** — glowing red eyes in the darkness
- Chase the player on foot, attack on contact (1 HP)
- 5 HP — vulnerable to ramming attacks
- Ramming damage scales with your speed!

### Bombs 💣
- Dropped by planes
- 10 damage (reduce to 1 by holding right-click)
- Can be dodged with sharp turns

## ⛏️ Resource Gathering

### Trees 🌴
- 100 HP, drop **60 wood** when chopped
- HP bar visible during combat
- Falling animation when destroyed
- **Warning:** Ramming without a tool damages you!

### Stones 🥌
- 200 HP, drop **1 stone** when mined
- Found in all biomes (2-4 per chunk)
- Mining with tools is safe and fast

### Iron Ore ⛏️
- 300 HP, drop **1 iron ore**
- 4 spawn per chunk
- Rusty brown appearance

### Tungsten Ore ⚒️
- 400 HP, drop **1 tungsten ore**
- 1 spawn per chunk (rare!)
- Shiny blue-glow appearance

### Carbon ⚫
- Found in Sky Chests
- Used for future crafting recipes

### Aetherium 💎
- Found in Low Aether Chests
- God-tier material for endgame crafting

## 📥 How to Play (Detailed)

1. **Download** the `albatrosssurvival.html` file:
   - Click the file in this repository
   - Click **"Download raw file"** (or right-click → Save As)

2. **Open** in a modern browser:
   - ✅ Chrome (recommended — best performance)
   - ✅ Edge (works great)
   - ✅ Firefox (solid choice)
   - ⚠️ Safari — may have limitations

3. **Click** the game window to lock your mouse

4. **Take off:** Hold `W` + `SPACE` — run until you can lift off!!

5. **Explore:**
   - Fly low to find villages and chests
   - Climb to 1000m for cloud platforms and Iron Peaks
   - Reach 1500m for the Low Aether Crystal Metropolis

6. **Survive:**
   - Day = hunting planes patrol the skies
   - Night = hunters emerge from the darkness

7. **Enchant:** Find an ✨ item and press `K` to transform your wings!
